
# UNIDEV Landing Page

A bold, minimalist black & white landing page for the UNIDEV game developers group.

## Layout & Design
- **Full-screen hero** with pure black background and white text
- **UNIDEV logo** prominently centered (using the uploaded logo file), large and commanding
- **Three event cards** arranged in a row beneath the logo, each featuring:
  - **UNICON** — Indie Game Show
  - **UNIJAM** — Gamejam for Members
  - **UNICODE** — Game Developers Conference
- Cards will have a clean monochrome style with subtle borders/hover effects
- Fully responsive — cards stack vertically on mobile

## Vibe
- Stark black & white palette, no color accents
- Bold typography, geometric feel matching the logo aesthetic
- Smooth subtle animations on scroll/hover
